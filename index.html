Privacy Policy

Multiplication AI
Effective Date: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Multiplication AI ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children.

Summary: Multiplication AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

1. Information We Collect

Multiplication AI does not collect any personal information from its users. The app is designed to function entirely offline without requiring any personal data.

The only data generated by the app is:

  • Learning Progress: Your multiplication practice history, scores, and performance statistics.
  • App Preferences: Your chosen settings such as language, selected numbers, sound preferences, theme, and character selection.
  • High Scores: Your best speed mode times.
  • Daily Statistics: The number of correct answers per day (last 30 days).

All of this data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

2. Children's Privacy

Multiplication AI is designed for children and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

  • We do not collect personal information from children or any other users.
  • We do not require user accounts or registration.
  • We do not use tracking, analytics, or behavioral profiling.
  • The app does not contain social features, chat, or user-generated content.

3. In-App Purchases

Multiplication AI offers a one-time premium purchase through Google Play's billing system. This transaction is handled entirely by Google Play and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. This service is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

The app does not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any other third-party services that collect user data.

5. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data breach from our end. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

6. Advertising

The free version of Multiplication AI may display ads. Premium users enjoy an ad-free experience. Any advertising is handled by third-party ad networks, which may collect device identifiers for the purpose of serving ads. Please refer to the respective ad network's privacy policy for more details.

7. Permissions

Multiplication AI requests minimal device permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required for in-app purchases through Google Play and for displaying ads in the free version.

The app does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
